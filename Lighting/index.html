Light X Light Y Light Z

Light Color: R G B

Spotlight Direction: X Y Z

Controls: WASD to move, QE to rotate view, Mouse to look around, +/- to zoom

Notes:

1. The maze was supposed to have a minimap, but everything broke every time I tried to implement it, so I removed it.
2. The end is supposed to be a 5x5 flat area, which has a chest in the middle.
3. The collision detection is a little finnicky so when you hit a wall you'll just stop. Try using the mouse to move the camera side to side to "squeeze past" the collision detection.
4. Get fairly close to the chest and `Shift + Left-Click` the chest for the end-screen.
